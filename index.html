About LAUTECH

Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), Ogbomoso, is a public university established in 1990. Named after Chief Ladoke Akintola, the former premier of the Western Region of Nigeria, the institution has grown to become one of Nigeria's leading universities of technology.

The university started with just 436 students in 1990 and has expanded to accommodate over 30,000 students across various faculties. LAUTECH is renowned for its excellence in engineering, technology, and health sciences education.

LAUTECH's mission is to advance the frontiers of learning, break new grounds in teaching and research, and produce graduates who are academically sound and practically oriented.

It hols the prestige of the Best State University of Technology in Nigeria, the school was ranked aeound the 1501+ range in the recent TIMES rating. It works to actualise its motto "Enhancing Self Reliance"

How to Calculate LAUTECH Aggregate Score

To be eligible for admission, your aggregate score is calculated using a 80:20 ratio of your UTME score and O'level grades respectively. The ratio division was used for the 2024/2025 Admission

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. UTME Score (80%)
    Formula: (Your JAMB Score / 400) × 80
  2. O'Level Grades (20%)
    LAUTECH uses five relevant subjects to determine your O'level contribution.
    Each grade is assigned a point as follows:
    • A1 = 6 points
    • B2 = 5 points
    • B3 = 4 points
    • C4 = 3 points
    • C5 = 2 points
    • C6 = 1 point
    Total O'level score is calculated over 30, then converted to 20%.

Example Calculation (For Mr. Ibrahim):

Let's assume Mr. Ibrahim has:

  • UTME Score: 400
  • O'level Grades: A1 in 5 relevant subjects

UTME Contribution:
400 ÷ 400 × 80 = 80

O'level Contribution:
A1 = 6 points × 5 subjects = 30/30*20 = 20

Total Aggregate Score = 80 + 20 = 100

This is Mr. Ibrahim's aggregate score which will be used to determine his eligibility for his chosen course.
